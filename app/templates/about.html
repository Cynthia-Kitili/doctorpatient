About Daktari App

This is an application that makes it possible to access doctor's services
by bringing the doctor closer to the patient.
Patients can log into this application and post their issues
The doctors who specialise in that field can leave a comment for the patient.
The patient can also be able to able to find the contacts of doctors who specialise in different fields and the hospitals they work for.
Through this information, patients can reach the Doctors in a faster and efficient way.
